Your Monetization Partner

WiConnect is a Wi-Fi Monetization as a Service (MaaS) provider that maximizes revenue generation from public Wi-Fi networks

What's MaaS?

MaaS is a unique approach to generating revenue from Wi-Fi networks on behalf and in support of venue owners and city managers.

Rather than selling or licensing platforms for the purpose of revenue generation, WiConnect takes all the risk and leverages its platform, monetization tools and expertise to maximize revenue generation.
